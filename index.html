Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our services, appointments, and care philosophy.

Do you treat speech delay or only hearing loss?

We are a fully integrated **Speech & Hearing Clinic**. We treat a comprehensive range of speech-language pathologies, including developmental delays, stuttering, voice disorders, and communication challenges related to conditions like Autism and ADHD, alongside our audiology services.

How long does a first diagnostic session take?

The initial comprehensive diagnostic session typically lasts between 60 to 90 minutes. This allows for a detailed evaluation of your or your child's communication profile and a discussion of preliminary findings.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Coverage varies widely depending on your health plan. We recommend contacting your provider directly to confirm coverage for "Speech-Language Pathology" and "Audiology Services." We will provide all necessary documentation for reimbursement.
